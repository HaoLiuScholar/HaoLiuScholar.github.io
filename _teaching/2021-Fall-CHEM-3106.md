Course Description 
======
Students will learn hands-on laboratory techniques and practical knowledge of fundamental organic reactions regarding various functional groups such as alcohols, aromatic compounds, ketones, aldehydes, amines, esters, and carboxylic acids. Students will continue using fundamental organic techniques, laboratory safety skills, and instruments that were introduced and emphasized in CHEM 3105.
