Course Description 
======
Students will gain experience with fundamental organic techniques, such as distillation, recrystallization, thin layer chromatography (TLC), and develop a working knowledge of infrared (IR) spectroscopy. Students will be introduced to laboratory practices of organic chemistry by conducting organic reactions (addition, elimination, substitution, etc) that have been taught in the classroom.
